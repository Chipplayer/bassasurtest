About Bassasur

Bassasur is an Indian Independent Music Promotional Channel which features and promotes Music that comes under the genre of Trap and Future Bass Music..

BassAsur is made to promote awesome music from wonderful artists all across the globe regardless of genre of music they produce. I will try to promote music on BassAsur as diverse as possible. Genre of music promoted on BassAsur will range from mostly Trap (because I love it) to Dubstep to Trance to few occasional uploads of Chill, Indie, Rap, Pop, etc.
